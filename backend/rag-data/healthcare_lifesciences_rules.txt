Profile: Healthcare / Clinical Research / Life Sciences
Healthcare resumes should prioritize patient safety, clinical documentation, medical terminology, care coordination, and regulatory compliance.
Clinical research roles should show protocol support, data collection, site coordination, adverse-event documentation, GCP, and audit readiness.
Pharmacovigilance resumes should highlight case processing, safety databases, MedDRA coding, narratives, timelines, and quality review.
Laboratory and quality roles should show testing methods, SOPs, equipment, QMS, CAPA, validation, and accuracy.
Strong bullets quantify patient or case volume, turnaround time, quality, compliance, or process improvements without exposing protected data.
