Profile: Electrical / Electronics Engineer
Highlight power systems, circuit design, control systems, PLC, SCADA, electrical maintenance, testing, safety, and relevant standards.
Power roles should show distribution, transformers, switchgear, relay protection, load studies, commissioning, and preventive maintenance.
Electronics roles may emphasize embedded systems, microcontrollers, PCB design, signal processing, VLSI, MATLAB, or Simulink.
Strong bullets quantify capacity, voltage level, downtime, energy savings, fault reduction, maintenance turnaround, or project value.
Include drawings, calculations, inspections, root-cause analysis, vendor coordination, and safety compliance where supported.
Software and tools such as AutoCAD Electrical, ETAP, MATLAB, PLC platforms, or SCADA should be tied to real deliverables.
