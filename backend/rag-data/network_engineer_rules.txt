Profile: Network Engineer / Network Administrator
Prioritize TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, routing, switching, VLANs, VPNs, firewalls, LAN/WAN, wireless, and network monitoring.
Include Cisco or other vendor platforms, configuration, troubleshooting, change management, documentation, and uptime responsibilities.
Strong bullets quantify sites, devices, users, incidents, latency, availability, migration scope, or troubleshooting time.
Show experience with packet analysis, access control, redundancy, backup configuration, and escalation procedures when relevant.
Certifications such as CCNA or CCNP should be listed only when actually earned.
