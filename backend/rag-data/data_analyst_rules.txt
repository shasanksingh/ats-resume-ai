Data Analyst resumes should show SQL, Excel, Power BI, Tableau, dashboarding, data cleaning, KPI tracking, stakeholder reporting, and statistical analysis.
Strong analyst bullets explain the business question, dataset size or source, method used, and decision impact.
Use terms such as exploratory data analysis, cohort analysis, funnel analysis, segmentation, forecasting, A/B testing, variance analysis, and data visualization when truthful.
Projects should describe data sources, transformation steps, visual outputs, and measurable outcomes such as reduced reporting time or improved conversion insight.
Entry-level analyst profiles should include portfolio dashboards, cleaned datasets, SQL queries, Excel models, and clear business recommendations.
Avoid vague phrases like worked on reports; prefer created a Power BI dashboard tracking revenue, retention, churn, or operational KPIs.
