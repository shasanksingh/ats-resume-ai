Digital Marketing resumes should highlight SEO, SEM, Google Analytics, GA4, Google Ads, Meta Ads, email marketing, content strategy, conversion rate optimization, CRM, and campaign management.
Strong marketing bullets include budget, audience, channel, experiment, KPI, and business outcome such as conversion rate, ROAS, CPL, CTR, organic traffic, or lead volume.
ATS systems often match keyword research, landing pages, A/B testing, marketing automation, HubSpot, Mailchimp, social media marketing, paid search, paid social, and lead generation.
Portfolio projects should show campaign objective, targeting strategy, creative or content approach, measurement setup, and optimization actions.
Entry-level profiles can include simulated campaigns, SEO audits, GA4 setup, content calendars, email sequences, and analytics dashboards.
Avoid buzzwords without metrics; replace managed campaigns with launched paid search campaigns and improved qualified leads or reduced cost per lead when truthful.
