Profile: Sales / Business Development / Customer Success
Sales resumes should show pipeline generation, revenue, quota attainment, conversion, deal size, territory, and relationship management.
Business development bullets should explain prospecting channels, partnerships, proposals, negotiation, and market expansion.
Customer success resumes should show onboarding, adoption, renewals, retention, churn reduction, support coordination, and customer health.
Name CRM tools such as Salesforce or HubSpot when tied to reporting, forecasting, or workflow ownership.
Strong bullets quantify accounts, meetings, opportunities, renewal value, satisfaction, response time, or expansion revenue.
