Recruiters scan resumes for role alignment, recent experience, clear job titles, measurable outcomes, and readable formatting.
The top third of the resume should quickly communicate target role, core skills, and highest-value accomplishments.
Every recommendation must preserve truthfulness. Do not invent employers, dates, certifications, degrees, metrics, or tools.
Strong bullet points explain action, technical method, and result in one concise sentence.
Weak resumes list duties without impact, use vague claims, or bury relevant skills in dense paragraphs.
