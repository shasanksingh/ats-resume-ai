Profile: Human Resources / Recruiter / Talent Acquisition Specialist
Highlight sourcing, screening, interviewing, onboarding, payroll, employee engagement, performance management, HR operations, and compliance.
Strong recruiting bullets quantify roles filled, time to hire, offer acceptance, pipeline size, sourcing conversion, or stakeholder satisfaction.
HR operations bullets may show employee count, payroll accuracy, policy implementation, grievance handling, retention, or process improvements.
Name ATS, HRIS, job portals, assessment platforms, and reporting tools only when used.
Keep candidate and employee data confidential while showing scale and outcomes.
