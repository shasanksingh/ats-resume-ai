Profile: DevOps / Cloud / Site Reliability Engineer
Prioritize AWS, Azure, or GCP services; Docker; Kubernetes; Terraform; CI/CD; Linux; monitoring; and incident response.
Strong bullets quantify deployment frequency, recovery time, availability, infrastructure cost, provisioning time, or alert reduction.
Describe infrastructure as code, release pipelines, container orchestration, secrets, networking, logging, and environment standardization.
SRE resumes should include SLI/SLO ownership, observability, capacity planning, postmortems, on-call response, and reliability improvements.
Avoid generic tool inventories without showing production scale, operational responsibility, or outcomes.
