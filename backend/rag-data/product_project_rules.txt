Profile: Product Manager / Project Manager / Business Analyst
Product resumes should show customer discovery, requirements, roadmap prioritization, experimentation, adoption, retention, and business outcomes.
Project resumes should show scope, schedule, budget, risks, dependencies, stakeholders, vendors, governance, and delivery status.
Business analyst resumes should highlight requirements gathering, process mapping, user stories, acceptance criteria, UAT, and data-backed decisions.
Strong bullets quantify users, revenue, adoption, cycle time, cost, delivery speed, backlog size, or stakeholder impact.
Avoid vague coordination claims; clarify the decision made, artifact produced, team influenced, and result achieved.
